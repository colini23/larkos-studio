Tabitha Rice

Exploring emotion through color and form.

View Works

Tabitha Rice is a Sydney based artist whose work explores abstract, multi-sensory experiences through acrylic and layered textures.

Her practice is rooted in instinct and materiality, using texture, movement, and repetition to embody emotional states rather than depict them. Each work exists as a sensory translation — where perception distorts and feeling takes form.

Her pieces invite a physical response, engaging the viewer beyond the visual and into the realm of sensation.

Dream Residue

Dream Residue explores the afterimage of experience. The soft, disjointed fragments that remain once a moment has passed.

Inspired by dreams, overstimulation, and emotional imprint, each piece exists in a space between recognition and ambiguity. Forms dissolve, textures build and erode, and meaning shifts depending on how you meet it.

Nothing is fixed. Everything lingers.
